About Us

MixORG OÜ focuses on Cyber Security especially with a focus on healthcare, as both are key strengths of Estonia as a country. Also, Estonia is a leader in e-health and e-governance, and the foundation of the same is built on solid cryptography. MixORG OÜ leverages the local expertise with global experience of working with some of the leading healthcare organizations to make them aware of the security risks by providing them high quality training programs and security audits.

Our Services

  • - Cyber Security Training program for Hospitals and Healthcare Organizations
  • - Security Audits for Hospitals and Healthcare Organizations ( MedTech companies)
